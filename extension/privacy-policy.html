Privacy Policy – Daily Dispo Deals Deal Checker

Last updated: [Add date]

Overview

Daily Dispo Deals Deal Checker helps you evaluate cannabis dispensary deals by overlaying value badges on menu pages. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Stored Locally (Your Device Only)

  • Device/Install ID – A random identifier for future account linking. Stored only on your device.
  • Cached results – Analyzed menu data may be cached locally so badges can be re-applied without re-analyzing.

Data We May Receive

When you click Analyze on a supported menu page, the extension may send to our servers:

  • Product names, prices, weights, and categories from the page
  • Install ID (not linked to your identity)
  • Extension version

We do not collect your name, email, browsing history, or payment information.

How We Use Data

To provide scoring, badges, and comparison features; to improve the extension; and when required by law.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data only with service providers or when required by law.

Your Choices

Don't click Analyze to avoid sending data. Uninstalling the extension removes locally stored data.
